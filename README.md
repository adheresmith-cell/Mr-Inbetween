# Mr-Inbetween
  本项目基于电视剧《Mr Inbetween》，主要用于研究以下内容：
  This project is based on the TV series "Mr Inbetween" and is mainly used to research the following content：
- Killer Price Economics 杀手价格经济学 
- Killer Supply Chain Research 杀手供应链研究
- Costs and benefits of professionalization 职业化成本与受益
- occupational labor protection 职业劳动保护
- Killer Cost Statistics 杀手成本核算
- against violence 反对暴力
- pursue fairness and justice 追求公平正义
- Analysis of Phenomenal Killer Events 现象级杀手事件分析 
- Crowdfunding Killer Platform 众筹杀手平台
- Public welfare actions 公益行动
- Identification and protection of innocent people  无辜人员鉴定及保护
- Witness Protection 证人保护
